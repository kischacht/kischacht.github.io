Kira Schacht About me

Studies data driven journalism at the Technical University of Dortmund. Leads the Meetup Journocode together with Marie-Louise Timcke.
Did an internship with BR Data and worked in Text Mining at DoCMA. Works at RP Online App-Team and does freelance data projects.
